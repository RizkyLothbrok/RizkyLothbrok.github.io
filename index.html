Meeting 11 - Listening: Short Conversation on Idioms

In this meeting, we will focus on improving our listening skills by exploring short conversations related to idioms. Idioms are expressions that have figurative meanings different from their literal interpretations. Understanding idioms is essential for effective communication in English. Let's dive into the conversation and explore some commonly used idiomatic expressions.

Meeting 12 Listening - Long Conversation: Who, What, Where, When, Why, and How

The long conversation in Meeting 12 focuses on gathering information using the six key questions: Who, What, Where, When, Why, and How. These questions are fundamental in understanding the details and context of any situation. Let's explore each question and its significance in extracting meaningful information.
